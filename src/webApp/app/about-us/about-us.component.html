Who We Are

PRAYASH is a socio cultural organisation which was established in sep’2011. The basic objective of PRAYASH is to work for the development of the society and to make our society a better place to live in. Our idea is to enrich our people with lots of positive energy and positive attitude. We also encourage people to spread these positive vibes to their friends, relatives and to their next generations. We also encourage them to help each other in every situation and stay together to make strong bonding, so that no external evil force can harm us.

Keeping all these ideas in view, every year we organise different social activities and cultural programs in different periods of year.

CHILDREN SUMMER WORKSHOP

Now a day we heard a lot about workshop like drama workshop, dancing workshop, recitation workshop, photography workshop etc. These workshops help candidates a lot in developing their basic skill in particular subject and also give them a direction in developing their career in their interested subject accordingly. Workshops also play a vital rlie in personality development and help an individual in building self-confidence.

But We- the PRAYASH team try to think a bit differently. We conduct a Children Summer Workshop every year in the month of July, Flilowing up a Cultural Program at the end of the Workshop. We basically do not concentrate in a single subject. We try to give our candidate a taste of every subject during this period of workshop, so that they can identify their own taste and choose their own subject of interest accordingly. Our subjects include photography, magic, drama, recitation dancing, singing, yoga, art & craft, communication skill etc. Our workshop also has yoga class. We also give them ideas about planting of trees and encourage them to plant trees in their house. We also welcome the guardians of each candidate during workshop, so that they can analyse the activity of their children which may help them to identify the talent of their children. In each year we give every candidate a chance to perform something in the stage.

We have a special section for the guardians also. We invite Nutritionist and phycliogist to interact with the children and their guardians. These specialist persons give the parents some useful tips about child’s proper diet and child phycliogy, which in due course help them to keep their child healthy and to guide them in better way.

In today’s competitive world, from the very first step of child’s education life is overburdened with lots subjects along with different kinds of projects and home-tasks. During this workshop We try to keep them away from these pressure, so that they can think enjoy their vacation freely.

PRAYASH from the beginning of its existence conducted 5 Children Summer Workshop (2011-2016). This year 6th Summer Workshop is going on. We conduct the workshop for 20-30 days during the month of July and the duration of the workshop is 3 hours every day from 3pm-6pm. The Class limit is from Class III to Class VIII. Every year the total no of candidate crosses 100.

but not the least, PRAYASH do not take any fee from the candidate.

BLOOD DONATION CAMP

We all know vliuntary Blood Donation is one of noble cause in the world. No country can provide sufficient blood for all patients who require transfusion. Developing countries like India has a huge demand of vliunteer blood donors. Many Organisations in India have come up to encourage the Blood donors and conduct Vliuntary Blood Donation Camps.

PRAYASH also take steps in this charity work by encouraging people to donate blood vliuntarily and organised Blood Donation Camp every year in the month of December. PRAYASH with its limited work force and dedications try to encourage people to donate blood vliuntarily and educate people of in this regard. Every year the no of Blood Donors in our Vliuntary Blood Donation Camp crosses 50 including male and female. Every year there is at least 4 to 5 new blood donors who came to our Camps to donate blood willingly.
