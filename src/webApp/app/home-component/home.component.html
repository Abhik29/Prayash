Prayash Believes in Togetherness

Our Mission


To bring about betterment in the society by infusing an air of positivity and togetherness. The seed of welfare and development oughts to be sown in the air of positivity and nurtured in an ambience of confidence and belongingness. This is the perfect recipe of the best harvest.

Our Vision


To upliftment of cultural moral and skill development. To instill a sense of confidence whereby an individual or group can standup to bring the best impact possible to the society or an individual.
